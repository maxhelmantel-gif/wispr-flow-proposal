Why this is different

Not a stunt. Something that stays.

Plenty of people walk a Camino. Far fewer leave something useful behind. We build a working AI tool for Stichting MIND, a Dutch charity for youth mental health, and give it to them for free.

The walk ends. The tool stays.

Software worth the walk.

We build the tool with MIND, around their real needs, so it takes routine work off their team and frees them up for the young people who count on them. We build it on the move, by voice, with AI doing the heavy lifting.
