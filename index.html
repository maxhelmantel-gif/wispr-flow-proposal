What we do

The world as our office.

We're Caesar, Stijn and Max, three founders in our twenties. We build like everyone our age: heads down, screens on. This summer we do it differently. We walk 120 km of the Camino and build as we go, using Wispr to work by voice so we stay present, not buried in a screen.

Why MIND

MIND is the whole point.

Stichting MIND is a Dutch organisation for mental wellbeing. We build an app for them, so young people find the foundation more easily and reach for it every day. The more they use it, the further MIND's impact reaches.

The walk ends. The app keeps going.
